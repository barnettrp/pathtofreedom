About Path to Freedom

Grace-based coaching rooted in truth, compassion, and transformation

Our Story

A Journey of Grace and Freedom

Path to Freedom Coaching was born from a deep understanding of the struggle many men face with porn and sexual addiction. We've witnessed firsthand the pain of cycles of shame, the exhaustion of trying harder, and the isolation that comes with this battle.

But we've also witnessed something far more powerful: the transformative grace of God that brings true, lasting freedom. Not through shame or willpower, but through understanding our stories, healing our wounds, and experiencing God's unconditional love.

Our mission is to walk alongside men on their journey to freedom, offering grace-based coaching that honors their dignity, addresses the root causes of addiction, and points them toward the abundant life Christ offers.

The Grace Model

Our approach is grounded in the grace model of recovery, which recognizes that lasting change comes through God's love, not shame or behavior modification alone.
