Quick Answers

How quickly can I start coaching?

After your free consultation, we can typically schedule your first coaching session within a week, depending on your availability and our schedule.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All communications, whether by email, phone, or during coaching sessions, are completely confidential. We take your privacy very seriously.

What if I'm not sure coaching is right for me?

That's exactly what the free consultation is for! It's a no-pressure conversation where we can explore whether our approach is a good fit for your needs.

Can I contact you in a crisis?

While we respond to emails within 24 hours, we are not a crisis service. If you're in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, please call 988 (Suicide Prevention Lifeline) or go to your nearest emergency room.
