Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each coaching session?

Standard sessions are 60 minutes. The free consultation is 30 minutes.

Are sessions conducted online or in-person?

All sessions are conducted via secure video conferencing for your convenience and privacy.

Is coaching confidential?

Yes, all coaching sessions are completely confidential. I maintain strict privacy standards.

Do you accept insurance?

I'm not currently in-network with insurance providers, but I can provide receipts for potential reimbursement. Some HSA/FSA accounts may cover coaching services.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

I require 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid session fees.

How is coaching different from therapy?

Coaching is future-focused and action-oriented, helping you move forward. While I may explore past wounds, I'm primarily focused on creating positive change. If you need clinical therapy, I can provide referrals.
