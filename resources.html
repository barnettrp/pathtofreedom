Recommended Books

Essential reading for understanding grace-based recovery

"The Cure: What if God Isn't Who You Think He Is and Neither Are You?" by John Lynch, Bruce McNicol, and Bill Thrall

A transformative look at living in grace versus performance-based Christianity. Essential for understanding the foundation of grace-based recovery.

"Unwanted: How Sexual Brokenness Reveals Our Way to Healing" by Jay Stringer

Explores how our sexual choices are shaped by our stories, providing a compassionate path toward understanding and freedom.

"Pure Desire" by Ted Roberts

A comprehensive guide to breaking free from sexual addiction through spiritual and practical tools.

"The Last Relapse: Realize Your Potential, Reclaim Intimacy, and Resolve the Root Issues of Porn Addiction" by Sathiya Sam

A research-based and Bible-backed approach to overcoming porn addiction by addressing the root causes and providing practical tools for lasting freedom.

"Outgrow Porn: Find Lasting Freedom Without Fighting an Exhausting Battle" by Drew Boa

A compassionate, growth-oriented approach to overcoming pornography addiction without the shame and exhausting battle mindset.
